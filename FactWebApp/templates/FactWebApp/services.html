What we offer

At FACT, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet all your architectural and construction needs. From innovative architectural design solutions to expert construction management and sustainable design consulting, our team is dedicated to delivering excellence in every project. With a focus on creativity, functionality, and sustainability, we strive to exceed our clients' expectations and create spaces that inspire, endure, and enrich lives.

Architecture Design

We offer comprehensive architectural design services tailored to meet your specific needs. Our team of experienced architects will work closely with you to develop innovative and sustainable design solutions that enhance the functionality, aesthetics, and value of your project.

Construction Management

Our construction management services are designed to ensure that your project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. From project planning and scheduling to contractor management and quality control, we'll oversee every aspect of the construction process to ensure a successful outcome.

Sustainable Design Consulting

We specialize in sustainable design consulting, helping clients minimize their environmental impact and maximize their energy efficiency. Our team will assess your project's environmental footprint and develop strategies to reduce energy consumption, conserve resources, and promote sustainability throughout the design and construction process.
